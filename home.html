What Is Baba Is You?

Baba Is You is an award-winning puzzle game where the rules are presented as blocks that the player can interact with while they play. By carefully manipulating them, the player can change the rules of the game to solve puzzles.

Where Can I Play Baba Is You?

Baba Is You is playable on a variety of platforms including Steam, Nintendo Switch, and Mobile.

How Was Baba Is You Developed?

Baba Is You was developed by created by Arvi Teikari, a Finnish independent developer. The idea for the game was inspired by the theme of the 2017 Nordic Game Jam which was "Not There". Prompting Tekari to imagine a block of ice next to lava but not melting, protected by text reading "Ice Is Not Melt". Tekari developed the game using Lua and MultiMedia Fusion 2.

Awards

Baba Is You was recieved with overwhelming critical acclaim, winning the following awards:

  • First place at the 2017 Nordic Game Jam.
  • "Best Student Game" and "Excellence In Design" at the 2018 IGF Awards.
  • "Best Indie Game" at the 2019 Golden Joystick Awards.
  • "Indie Game of the Year" at the Titanium Awards.
  • "Best Independent Game" at The Game Awards 2019.
  • "Outstanding Achievement in Game Design" during the 23rd Annual D.I.C.E. Awards.
  • "Best Design" and the "Innovation Award" at the 20th Game Developers Choice Awards.
